Why Bids Fail

Eligibility Misread or Missed Thresholds

Minimum experience, capacity, or financial metrics overlooked until post-submission disqualification.

Missing or Expired Mandatory Documents

Critical certificates, licenses, or notarized originals incomplete or outside compliance window.

Weak or Non-Compliant Consortium Structure

Unclear roles, missing guarantees, or partner disqualification discovered only after submission.

Insufficient Local Content Commitments

Employment, procurement, or partnership targets fail to meet tender thresholds or lack credibility.

ESG Narrative Gaps or Missing Frameworks

Environmental or social commitments lack specificity, measurable targets, or alignment with evaluator expectations.

Documentary Formality Defects

Missing signatures, incorrect notarization, wrong format, or unsigned declarations trigger automatic rejection.

Evaluator-Criteria Misalignment

Technical narrative fails to address evaluator scoring logic or leaves high-value criteria underclaimed.

Pricing Submitted Without Upstream Compliance Lock

Financial proposal advanced before eligibility and compliance risks are fully resolved.
